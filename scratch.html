Trail Running

Why I love Trail Running

Trail Running has become one of my all time favorite hobbies. I think everyone should get into it! If you don't love running yet, you just haven't gone far enough! Check out how far this guy went

  1. Exercise
    • Running twice a week is as effective as antidepressants
    • Trail running helps strenthen ligaments and joints
    • It can be really hard, and that's good for you!
  2. Location
    • You get to go to cool places
    • The views are better when you had to work for it
  3. Social
    • The trail running community is small but friendly
    • Running with friends is a great way to grow closer together

My goal is to eventually do a 50 miler. The prospect seems super daunting now but I believe I'm on my way!
